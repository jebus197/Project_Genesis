How trust is built

Trust is earned, never bought. Every score reflects real, verified outcomes:

Quality — accuracy of work, assessed by independent reviewers on every mission.

Reliability — meeting deadlines and commitments. Consistency over time.

Volume — sustained contribution. Not spikes — steady, ongoing work.

Effort — difficulty of tasks you succeed at. More complex work grows trust faster.

Your reputation is not one number. Trust is tracked per domain — you might be highly trusted for healthcare analysis but start from scratch on a coding task. Skills are earned through mission outcomes, not self-declared. See the audit trail for full trust history.
