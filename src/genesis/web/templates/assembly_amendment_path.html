How Decisions Become Binding

Stage 1 · Assembly Discussion Non-binding

Open deliberation and evidence exchange. This stage informs policy, but cannot directly ratify policy.

Stage 2 · Proposal + Ratification Chambers Binding

Formal amendment entry, chamber panel selection, proposal vote, and ratification vote under constitutional constraints.

Stage 3 · Challenge / Verification Binding

Challenge window and chambered challenge path. Entrenched provisions continue through cooling-off and confirmation before apply.
