Proof of Concept mode

Genesis is in Proof of Concept mode. All data shown across the platform is demonstration data. The platform transitions to live operations at First Light — a financial sustainability trigger, not a headcount counter. Only verified human activity drives the mission volume that generates revenue.

First Light conditions

First Light is achieved when commission revenue sustainably covers operating costs:

  • Revenue ≥ 1.5× monthly costs
  • 3-month reserve fund accumulated
